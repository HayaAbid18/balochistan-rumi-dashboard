## TEMPLATE USAGE GUIDE

### **Timing**
- **Group 1 (Non-onboarded)**: Send Templates 1-5, staggered (2-3 days apart)
- **Group 2 (Re-engagement)**: Send Templates 6-12, once per week

### **Personalization Tips**
- Add teacher's name if you have it: "Hi Saila!"
- Mention school if you have it: "Great work at [School Name]"
- Reference their specific subject if known: "For math teachers..."

### **Channel**
- **WhatsApp**: Primary (all templates above)
- **SMS fallback**: Remove emojis & shorten (keep under 160 chars)

### **Response Rates Expected**
- **Cold outreach (Group 1)**: 5-15% reply rate
- **Re-engagement (Group 2)**: 15-30% reply rate

### **Success Metrics to Track**
1. Reply rate (% who respond)
2. Onboarding completion (% who register)
3. Feature usage (% who create 1st lesson plan)
4. Retention (% active 7 days later)
